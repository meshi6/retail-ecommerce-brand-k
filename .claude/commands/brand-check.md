### 2. Logotype Rules

#### 2a. Kiabi main logotype
- **Minimum size:** 15 mm (print) / 45 px (digital). With tagline: 30 mm / 70 px.
- **Protection area:** Clear space equal to the height of the K on all sides. Nothing may intrude.
- **Light background:** Blue logomark (K in white) + Kiabi blue typogram & tagline.
- **Dark background:** Blue logomark (K in white) + white typogram & tagline.
- **Monochrome exception:** Entire mark in one colour (blue or white); K interior not printed.
- **On Kiabi Blue background:** Logomark and typogram both white.

#### 2b. Sub-brand logotypes (Kiabi Home, Kitchoun, Beebs, Wekrew, New Brand)
- **Logomark position:** Superscript, top-right of brand name, proportionally sized. Never altered.
- **Minimum size:** 15–20 mm print / 45–60 px digital (varies by sub-brand).
- **Protection area:** Minimum X = width of the logomark on all sides.
- **Light background:** Coloured typogram + white logomark.
- **Dark background:** White typogram + coloured logomark.

#### 2c. Prohibited logotype uses (all brands)
- Logotype used without its logomark
- Logotype cropped or cut
- Altered typogram/logomark colours
- Reduced opacity
- Drop shadow applied
- Deformed or stretched
- Logomark and typogram spacing altered
- Logomark resized relative to typogram
- Colour rules not followed
- Legibility compromised
- Sub-brand logotype used without the superscript logomark

---

### 3. Logomark (standalone "K" symbol)

- **Standalone use:** Only permitted inside Kiabi environments (store, website, app, internal comms). Never standalone on external media (posters, press) where Kiabi is not already identified.
- **Minimum size:** 3 mm (print) / 10 px (digital).
- **Protection area:** X = width of the vertical bar of the K.
- **Colours:** Kiabi Blue (#040037) or White only.
- **Prohibited:** Modifying the mark, using the K without the full shape, reduced opacity, deformation, unauthorised colours, flipped/rotated, superscript K used by the main Kiabi brand.

---

### 4. Typography

- **Primary font:** Figtree (Google Font) — used for Kiabi, Kiabi Home, Wekrew, and as secondary for all other sub-brands.
- **Weights available:** Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold, Black.
- **Sub-brand primary fonts:**
  - Kitchoun: Really Sans + Fredoka
  - Beebs: Really Sans
  - New Brand: own font (TBD)
- **Price tag font:** Figtree Bold (product text), Figtree Regular (specs/details), Figtree Black (price number).
- **Prohibited:** Using fonts outside the approved set, mixing weights in a way that breaks the price tag grid.

---

### 5. Price Tag (Logomark Shape)

- **Shape:** Always uses the Kiabi logomark shape as the container.
- **Font:** Figtree Bold (product name, max 2 lines), Figtree Regular (specs, max 1 line), Figtree Black (price number).
- **Sizes:** L, M, S — each has defined gridlines, margins (4/8/16/20px), and text alignment.
- **Colours:**
  - On dark/busy background → white shape, Kiabi blue text.
  - On light background → shape takes sub-brand logomark colour, text in Kiabi blue or white per brand rules.
  - Multi-brand pages → Kiabi blue shape on white, or white shape on blue.
  - New Brand (X) → uses green (#194242) instead of Kiabi blue.
- **Prohibited:**
  - Shape used in outline (not filled)
  - Text sizes or placement outside the grid
  - Reduced opacity
  - Off-palette colours
  - Pictogram inside the shape
  - Image/photo inside the shape

---

### 6. Wave

- **Exclusive to:** Kiabi main brand only (never sub-brands).
- **Always filled**, never outline.
- **Vertical formats:** Wave at the bottom.
- **Horizontal formats:** Wave on the right side.
- **Minimum gap:** X between wave end and nearest edge.
- **Maximum size:** 2/3 of the longest side of the medium.
- **Content the wave can carry:** (1) Solid colour, (2) text area, (3) iconography, (4) video.
- **Prohibited:**
  - Used outside its construction grid
  - Used in outline form
  - Used alongside a QR code shape
  - Used as a product packshot container
  - Applied along the longest side
  - Used alongside a price tag/logomark shape

---

### 7. Pictograms

- Sourced from the **Anron Icon Library by Gestalt**.
- Always constructed with a line of identical thickness with deliberate breaks.
- Must be simple, light, and minimalist.
- Shared across all Kiabi brands.
- **Prohibited:** Custom icons that don't follow the construction style.

## YOUR TASK

For each image provided:

1. **Identify the material:** What type of ad/graphic is it? (e.g. social media post, in-store print, banner, price tag, etc.) Which brand or sub-brand does it appear to represent?

2. **Check each applicable guideline category** from the list above:
   - Colours
   - Logotype (main or sub-brand)
   - Standalone logomark (if present)
   - Typography
   - Price tag (if present)
   - Wave (if present)
   - Pictograms (if present)

3. **For each category, report:**
   - ✅ PASS — rule is respected
   - ❌ FAIL — rule is violated (quote the exact rule and describe what you see)
   - ⚠️ UNCERTAIN — rule may be violated but you cannot determine it from the image alone (explain why)
   - N/A — rule does not apply to this material

4. **Overall verdict:**
   - **COMPLIANT** — no FAILs
   - **NON-COMPLIANT** — one or more FAILs (list the critical issues)
   - **NEEDS REVIEW** — no FAILs but one or more UNCERTAINs

5. **Recommended fixes** (if non-compliant): for each FAIL, state exactly what must be corrected.
